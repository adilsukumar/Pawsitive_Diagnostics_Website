Applicability: India & Japan

The Dual-Market Bridge.

We aren't just solving a local issue. We are targeting a ₹26.1 Lakh Crore global opportunity bridging two uniquely desperate markets.

🇮🇳

India: The Rabies Crisis

India accounts for 36% of the world's rabies deaths. With millions of stray dogs and rapidly growing pet ownership, there is a massive lack of clinical infrastructure. We provide scalable early detection (PressureSense tracking swallowing patterns for hydrophobia) to assist government street-dog monitoring programs and local clinics.

🇯🇵

Japan: Premium Aging Pets

In Japan, pets outnumber children. The market is driven by owners seeking high-end, preventative geriatric care for their aging companions. Features like MotionSense (early arthritis detection) and TempSense cater directly to a high-willingness-to-pay demographic that demands proactive, precise medical insight.

The Collar

Hardware Anatomy

A custom-designed PCTG clinical collar packed with an array of biometric sensors.

Collar 3D Design

Custom PCB Layout

MPU 6050 (IMU)

Calculates rest, walking, and running. Detects scratching, head-shaking (ear infections), posture, and tracks seizures with sudden acceleration spikes.

GPS Tracking

Real-time location, speed, heading, and geofencing. Runs every 5 mins to save battery, or every 10ms in "lost dog mode".

Dual Piezo Sensors

Detects swallowing mechanisms against the throat to track hydration and hydrophobia. Identifies which dog is barking by feeling voicebox pressure.

INMP441 Microphone

Predicts 9 emotions from barks. Actively processes background noise to distinguish threat words, loud environments, and dangerous situations.

LDR & Adaptive LED

Detects hiding in dark places (sickness indicator). Automatically adjusts LED brightness. LEDs signal health status (Green=Healthy, Red=Sick).

Temp Sensor & MOSFET

Measures internal temperature spikes (fever/heatstroke). MOSFET shuts off GPS and unnecessary sensors when the dog is asleep to maximize battery life.
