"Build intelligent systems that can think, decide and act independently."

Pawsitive Diagnostics is not a passive sensor array. It is an autonomous agent living on the edge. It continuously monitors, processes data through onboard ML, and acts (triggers alerts, shifts LED states) without waiting for human intervention.

The Architecture

Four Intelligent Agents.

Operating independently to protect, diagnose, and alert.

🎙️

BarkSense AI

Autonomous acoustic analysis engine that monitors vocalizations to detect physiological distress, pain, and rabies symptoms.

Think: Analyze Audio Act: Alert on Pain
💨

AirSense AI

Proactive environmental monitoring. Continuously sniffs for hazardous gases, toxic leaks, and poisoning attempts.

Think: Monitor Air Quality Act: Evacuation Alarm
🏃

MotionSense AI

IMU-based biomechanical analysis mapping gait and posture to detect neurological disorders or severe arthritis.

Think: Track 6-Axis Motion Act: Flag Anomalies
🔬

SkinSense AI

UV Fluorescence visual diagnosis using Gemini Vision AI to identify hidden microbial infections under the fur.

Think: UV Image Scan Act: Gen Auto-Report
