Pawsitive Diagnostics is entirely aligned with Theme 3. We didn't build a passive sensor dashboard that requires human oversight. We engineered a self-governing agent living on the edge.

The Architecture

Four Intelligent Sub-Agents.

Operating independently to protect, diagnose, and act upon environmental and biological data.

🎙️

BarkSense AI

Autonomous acoustic analysis engine that monitors vocalizations to detect physiological distress, pain, and aggressive rabies symptoms.

Think: Analyze real-time audio frequencies.
Decide: Classify emotion utilizing ML models.
Act: Alert owners & authorities automatically.
💨

AirSense AI

Proactive environmental monitoring. Continuously sniffs for hazardous gases, toxic leaks, and targeted poisoning attempts in strays.

Think: Monitor VOCs and CO levels continuously.
Decide: Compare against biological safety baselines.
Act: Trigger evacuation alarms and log coordinates.
🏃

MotionSense AI

IMU-based biomechanical analysis mapping gait, posture, and tremors to detect severe arthritis or neurological decline.

Think: Track 6-Axis motion telemetry 24/7.
Decide: Identify anomalies in movement patterns.
Act: Compile veterinary reports and flag risks.
🔬

SkinSense AI

UV Fluorescence visual diagnosis using Vision AI to identify hidden microbial infections beneath the animal's fur.

Think: Process 365nm UV illuminated imagery.
Decide: Classify ringworm, dermatitis, or lesions.
Act: Auto-generate dermatological records.
