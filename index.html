The Architecture

Translating the silent language.

9 Proprietary AI agents operating independently to protect, diagnose, and act.

BarkSense AI

🎙️
  • • Filters background noise via digital mic
  • • Detects 6 emotions (Afraid, Angry, Happy, Normal, Pain, Sad)
  • • 87% accuracy on respiratory conditions

TempSense AI

🌡️
  • • Continuous skin temperature tracking
  • • Alerts to sudden spikes, fevers, or heatstroke
  • • Critical early-warning metric for infections

MotionSense AI

🏃
  • • IMU telemetry analysis via BMI270
  • • Flags heavy scratching, head-shaking, or seizures
  • • 92% accuracy detecting early-stage arthritis

SkinSense AI

🔬
  • • Photo/Video uploads for visual pattern recognition
  • • Instantly detects joint stiffness from walk videos
  • • Detachable UV LED reveals hidden fungus & infections

PressureSense AI

🗣️
  • • Tracks swallowing pressure
  • • Detects hydrophobia symptoms (Rabies)
  • • Detects signs of hoarseness and irritation

VitalSense AI

❤️
  • • Continuous pulse, heart rate & BP monitoring
  • • Real-time breathing pattern analysis
  • • Internal health always in check - 24/7

LocationSense AI

📍
  • • Real-time GPS tracking via U-Blox SAM-M10Q
  • • Automatic Geo-fencing & escape alerts
  • • SOS Lost Dog Mode shifts tracking to hyper-fast

LightSense AI

☀️
  • • Monitors sleep-wake cycles and circadian rhythm
  • • Smart Battery Saving: Shuts down heavy sensors when sleeping
  • • Dims collar LEDs at night

CombineSense AI

🧠
  • • One stop solution to all senses combined
  • • Get weekly-monthly-daily reports
  • • Track your dog's progress in real-time
