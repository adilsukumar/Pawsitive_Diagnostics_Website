The Silent Crisis

Animals cannot speak. When a pet or a stray dog falls ill, they suffer in silence. Humans only intervene when physical symptoms become severely apparent.

By the time a human notices the lethargy, the change in gait, or the respiratory distress, the disease has often progressed to a critical, expensive, or fatal stage. Healthcare is fundamentally reactive.

The Autonomous Guardian

We built Pawsitive Diagnostics not as a hardware tool, but as an Agentic System.

The collar operates independently. It continuously gathers acoustic, environmental, and biomechanical data. It processes this locally using lightweight AI models to think and decide if an anomaly exists.

Crucially, it acts autonomously. If it detects a toxic gas leak or signs of rabies, it immediately alerts authorities, updates the vet dashboard, and physically turns its LED indicator red to warn humans—all without requiring a human to press a button or check an app.

The Architecture

Four Intelligent Sub-Agents

How Pawsitive Diagnostics fulfills the "Think, Decide, Act" mandate across four critical health vectors.

🎙️
AUDIO ML

BarkSense AI

Autonomous acoustic analysis engine.

  • Think: Analyzes vocalization frequencies in real-time.
  • Decide: Classifies emotion (pain, aggression, normal) using ML.
  • Act: Logs distress events and notifies owners immediately upon crossing pain thresholds.
💨
ENV SENSOR

AirSense AI

Environmental hazard intelligence.

  • Think: Continuously sniffs ambient air for VOCs, CO, and hazardous gases.
  • Decide: Compares readings against breed-specific safety baselines.
  • Act: Triggers emergency evacuation alarms if toxic levels are detected.
🏃
BIOMECHANICS

MotionSense AI

IMU-based posture and gait analysis.

  • Think: Captures 6-axis gyro/accelerometer data 24/7.
  • Decide: Identifies neurological tremors, limping, or seizure patterns.
  • Act: Compiles movement reports and alerts vets to potential arthritis or neurological decline.
🔬
COMPUTER VISION

SkinSense AI

UV Fluorescence visual diagnosis.

  • Think: Uses 365nm UV light to reveal hidden microbial infections.
  • Decide: Employs Vision AI to classify ringworm, dermatitis, and lesions.
  • Act: Autogenerates preliminary dermatological reports for immediate veterinary review.
